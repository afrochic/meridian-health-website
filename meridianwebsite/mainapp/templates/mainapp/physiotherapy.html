Physiotherapy Services

Physiotherapy helps restore movement and function, maximises potential and helps prevent deterioration

or complications that may occur when someone is affected by injury, illness or disability.

Here at Meridian Health we offer one of the best and international standards services for you

Our physiotherapists do assessments, treatment and rehabilitation for:
  1. Physiotherapy for Children
  2. Physiotherapy after Breaking a Bone
  3. Physiotherapy After a Stroke
  4. Problems after Pregnancy
  5. Physiotherapy for Bone, Joint and Muscle Problems
  6. Physiotherapy After a Problem with Your Brain or Spinal Cord
  7. Physiotherapy Whilst in Hospital

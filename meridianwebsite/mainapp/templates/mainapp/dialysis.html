Dialysis Services

We run a nephrology clinic. To complement this unit at a time when statistics show a rising incidence of renal

impairment, we have established a dialysis unit to help the numerous patients whose clinical conditions daily

demand this service. With a dedicated team attached to the unit, it is available and accessible almost 24/7 and

it has restored hope and indeed, life to many. Comprehensive renal replacement therapy (dialysis) options are

available to people with chronic kidney disease.

For those with kidney failure or kidney disease and are in need of a dialysis, then at Meridian Hospital is the

place to be for we offer:

  • Chronic Kidney Disease (Renal Failure) support
  • Outpatient dialysis services
  • Home dialysis training and support for both home hemodialysis and peritoneal dialysis
  • Outpatient dialysis services
  • Temporary dialysis if you need treatment while traveling
  • In-center hemodialysis
